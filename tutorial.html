Overview

Welcome! In this project you will read sentences generated by Artificial Intelligence and rate their quality.

To perform this HIT you must be able to:

  1. Rank sentences by how well they simplify
  2. Rate sentences by how well they preserve meaning and literacy
  3. Identify sentences in an incorrect category
  4. Fix incorrectly labeled changes we made to sentences

Here's the big picture of what we're doing:

Ranking Sentences

The key to this task is ranking sentences by how well they simplify the original sentence. Click and drag sentences to re-order them.

Explanation: The highest sentences are the most "simple," regardless of whether the sentence is fluent or maintains the original meaning of the sentence

Rating Sentences

The other primary part of ths task is to rate sentences by how well they preserve meaning and fluency. One challenge is deciding how to score each sentence.

Here's some general rules for scores:

  • 100 - Only when the sentence is entirely fluent and preserves the entire meaning of the original sentence
  • 75 - The sentence is mostly fluent and the meaning is close to the original sentence
  • 50 - The sentence is somewhat fluent and the meaning is similar to the original sentence
  • 25 - The sentence still has some fluency but the meaning is lost
  • 0 - The sentence is completely unreadable

Most scores will lie somewhere in this range, feel free to give specific scores.

Categorizing Sentences

Our sentences are presented in three forms:

  • Deletion-focused - The AI attempted to simplify the sentence mainly by deleting words
  • Paraphrase-focused - A mix between deleting words and re-wording sets of words
  • Splitting-focused - Converts the sentence to multiple, simplified sentences

We already placed sentences in each of these categories, but sometimes these placements are wrong.

If the sentence is in the wrong caregory, click and drag the sentence to its correct category.

Using this editor, there's a couple important items to note:

  • To change a paraphrase - Click and drag the blue arrow at the end of the phrase.
  • To add a paraphrase - Select an area of the text that isn't bolded
  • To change a deletion or split - Click and drag the deletion () or split ()
  • To remove a deletion or split - Double click the deletion () or split ()
